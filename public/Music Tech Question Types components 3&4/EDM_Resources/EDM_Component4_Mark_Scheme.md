**Question 2: Sidechain Compression (4 marks)**

**Acceptable answers:**
*   **Setup:** Insert compressor on bass channel AND route Kick drum to Sidechain Input (Key Input).
*   **Attack:** Fast attack (0.1ms - 10ms) to catch the transient immediately OR slightly slower (10-30ms) to let some bass pluck through.
*   **Release:** Timed to the tempo/groove (e.g., 1/8th or 1/4 note), typically 50ms - 300ms, so volume recovers before next kick.
*   **Ratio:** High ratio (4:1 - 10:1 or higher) for noticeable "pumping" effect.
*   **Threshold:** Lower until gain reduction meter shows significant ducking (-10dB to -30dB).
**Award 1 mark for each valid point (Setup, Attack, Release, Pumping description), maximum 4.**
*Must reference "Kick" triggering the compressor.*

**Question 3: Kick Drum Mixing (3 marks)**

**Acceptable answers:**
*   **High Pass Filter (HPF):** 20Hz - 40Hz (Remove sub-rumble) -> **CUT**
*   **Fundamental / Lows:** 50Hz - 80Hz (Body/Thump) -> **BOOST**
*   **Mud / Boxiness:** 200Hz - 400Hz -> **CUT**
*   **Click / Beater:** 3kHz - 5kHz (Attack/Transient) -> **BOOST**
*   **Top End / Air:** 8kHz - 10kHz -> **BOOST** (Optional)
**Award 1 mark for each valid Frequency Range + Action pair, maximum 3.**

**Part (a) (1 mark)**
*   150Hz - 250Hz (Body) OR 2kHz - 5kHz (Crack/Snap)
**Award 1 mark.**

**Part (b) (2 marks)**

**Question 9: Pad Layering (2 marks)**

**Acceptable answers:**
*   **Filter Type:** High Pass Filter (HPF)
*   **Frequency:** 200Hz - 500Hz (to remove mud) OR Low Pass Filter at 2kHz - 5kHz (to sit behind leads).
*   **Context:** Specifically looking for filtering that creates space. "Not clashing with brightness" implies LPF to roll off highs OR HPF to clear mids.
**Award 1 mark for Filter Type + 1 mark for justifiable Frequency.**
